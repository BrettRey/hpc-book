### A2. Operationalize community grain (8/11 agents)
**Location:** Line 323 ("The answer is empirical, not stipulated")
**Problem:** "Empirical, not stipulated" reads as a dodge. Agents want operational criteria for convergent entrenchment.
**Fix:** Replace the single sentence with 4-5 sentences that operationalize convergence:
- Shared repair preferences across contexts (speakers converge on the same correction)
- Shared production distributions (similar frequencies given opportunity)
- Mutual intelligibility of the coupling (the would-be fires for both parties)

- End with: when these diagnostics diverge across populations, you have either nested communities (double modals) or divergent kinds (Irish English embedded inversion), and the maintenance structure tells you which.

## Priority B: Substantive (2-3 agents, moderate effort)

### B1. Expand HPC audit (Hacking, Dąbrowska)
**Location:** Lines 336-346
**Problem:** Compressed to 11 lines. Agents want to see the feedback loop *explaining* co-occurrence, not just listing it.
**Fix:** Add 4-6 sentences after line 338 ("Three properties co-occur systematically") spelling out how the maintenance loop couples them: entrenchment keeps form-value pairs stable; alignment keeps them shared; transmission keeps them available to new speakers. These three mechanisms predict that pairing, compatibility, and community convergence should cluster — because each mechanism reinforces the others. Where one fails, the others weaken (the *whose* case: intact pairing, intact compatibility, failing entrenchment → failing convergence).

### B2. Strengthen contingency/convergence passage (4/11 agents)
**Location:** Lines 352-358
**Problem:** Eye analogy is suggestive but doesn't specify functional pressures, and an essentialist can restate the same claim as "universal principle, variable parameters."
**Fix:** Add 3-4 sentences after the eye analogy specifying: the shared pressures are real-time production under memory constraints, compositional communication requiring predictable form-meaning mapping, and iterated transmission through acquisition bottlenecks. These pressures make *some* form-value coupling convergent; they don't dictate *which* couplings. That's the distinction: essentialism predicts universal contents; HPC predicts universal existence with contingent contents, and further predicts that tight couplings (subject-verb agreement) should be more convergent than loose ones (island constraints), because tight maintenance resists more perturbation.

### B3. Add one divergent prediction for relevance theory (Hacking, Jackendoff)
**Location:** Lines 326-330
**Problem:** The chapter claims different granularity but doesn't give a case where the frameworks diverge.
**Fix:** Add 2-3 sentences: Relevance theory predicts that repeated exposure to *any* relevance violation should produce habituation (the system recalibrates to the lower relevance baseline). The maintenance account predicts construction-specific satiation — some violations satiate, others resist, depending on entrenchment depth and competitor density (§15.6.1). The satiation data bear on this: if satiation is construction-specific rather than general, that favours maintenance over relevance recalibration.

### B4. Qualify the discreteness reference (Goldberg)
**Location:** Line 9
**Problem:** "Discrete grammaticality" invokes Ch 5's conclusion without flagging that discreteness is derived, not assumed.
**Fix:** Change "discrete grammaticality filtered through processing noise" to "grammaticality that Ch~5 argued is dynamically discrete — maintained sharp by the same mechanisms that could let it soften — filtered through processing noise." This both credits the earlier argument and signals that discreteness is earned, not stipulated.

### B5. Clarify licensing as emergent (Goldberg, Millikan)
**Location:** Line 21
**Problem:** "Licensing is what the grammar permits" risks smuggling back an autonomous grammar.
**Fix:** Add a qualifying clause: "Licensing is what the grammar permits — where 'the grammar' is the network of entrenched constructions and their inheritance links, not an autonomous rule system." This preserves the licensing/coupling distinction (which Heath praised) while blocking the autonomous-grammar reading.
