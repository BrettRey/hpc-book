**Strengths**  
Chapter 15 opens with a real human scene and immediate stakes: the red-pen correction of double copula (`chapters/chapter15.tex:6`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:10`). That is strong science-writing architecture: phenomenon first, theory second. The “mixed bin” bolt metaphor is also effective because it turns a statistical claim into a visualizable mistake (`chapters/chapter15.tex:56`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:64`). The chapter is at its best when mechanisms are shown as loops with actors, incentives, and timescales, especially in the three feedback-loop paragraphs for dialect/register/community (`chapters/chapter15.tex:103`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:122`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:133`). You also sustain continuity with Chapter 14’s “immune system” framing (`chapters/chapter14.tex:39`) and preserve the throughline that judgments are community-calibrated, not oracle outputs. The opening third has real narrative grip and gives readers a causal handle before abstraction arrives.

**Major Concerns**  
After the strong opening, the chapter too often names mechanisms instead of letting readers watch them happen. Terms accumulate quickly: “conditioning structure,” “latent mixture component,” “attractor basin,” “parameterization,” “inverse function” (`chapters/chapter15.tex:73`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:131`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:173`). Each term is defensible, but stacked together they reduce narrative traction and make the middle read like a conceptual taxonomy rather than a reportage of causal process. A second concern: there is only one vivid recurring case (double copula), while other examples are briefly gestured at and dropped (`chapters/chapter15.tex:101`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:154`). The planning notes explicitly called for additional nonfiction micro-scenes and constructional examples (`notes/chapter15_planning.md:21`, `notes/chapter15_planning.md:44`, `notes/ch15/board-ch15-plan-notes.md:136`). Third, the ending pivots back to philosophical defense (`chapters/chapter15.tex:190`) instead of cashing out an explicit methodological handoff to the gauntlet in Chapter 16 (`chapters/chapter16.tex:12`, `chapters/chapter16.tex:99`). So the chapter’s strongest empirical promise arrives early, then diffuses into abstraction.

**Priority Fixes**  
1. Build one continuous nonfiction case that runs through all three stabilizers. Start with the red-pen classroom scene, then show the same speaker in interview/professional discourse/community gatekeeping, so dialect-register-community are observed in one trajectory (`chapters/chapter15.tex:6`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:88`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:124`).  
2. Enforce a “term-to-scene” rule: every new technical term gets one concrete, falsifiable example in the next sentence. This will keep rigor while reducing conceptual drag (`chapters/chapter15.tex:73`, `chapters/chapter15.tex:173`).  
3. End with a short “what to measure next” bridge: 2–3 operational predictions tied directly to Chapter 16 observables (variance structure, lag order, conditioning variables) (`chapters/chapter16.tex:48`, `chapters/chapter16.tex:57`, `chapters/chapter16.tex:101`).
