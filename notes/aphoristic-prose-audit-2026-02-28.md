## Diagnosis

The good prose in these chapters explains with directness and specificity. The problems cluster at structurally prominent positions (openers, closers, transitions), which means they set the tonal register disproportionately even though they're a small fraction of total words.

The contrast with the metaphors paper is instructive: the good examples make complex claims in relaxed, multi-clause sentences that sound like someone thinking out loud. The flagged passages sound like someone who has finished thinking and is now performing the conclusion.
