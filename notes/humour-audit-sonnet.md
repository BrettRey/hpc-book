## Summary

### Funniest chapters (most moments + best quality):
1. **Chapter 4** — The mechanisms chapter has personality and swagger
2. **Chapter 8** — Failure modes; the "wobbling top" opening is perfect
3. **Chapter 11** — The wastebasket line and the mock-reassurance show confidence
4. **Chapter 1** — The Huddleston opening frame is memorable

### Driest chapters:
1. **Chapter 3** — Philosophical heavy lifting, nominalism debate, very earnest
2. **Chapter 9** — Countability (likely technical simulations)
3. **Chapter 2** — Essentialism dissection, appropriately diagnostic but sparse

### Biggest opportunities for improvement:
1. **Chapter 3, §3.1-3.2** — The lexeme obsession and gender/number material needs at least one moment of levity
2. **Chapter 2, §2.7** — "Why essentialism persists" is dry sociology; needs colour
3. **Chapter 5, post-hyperreals** — Basin structure material after the formal stuff could use one "here's where it gets interesting" moment
4. **Chapters 6-7** — Hard to assess fully, but the mechanisms material might benefit from more personality
5. **Chapters 9, 12-14** — Likely application-heavy; check whether the case studies have personality or are just diagnostic

### What's working:
- **Unexpected phrasing** ("more than most PhD students manage")
- **Structural irony** (frameworks contradicting themselves, verbless clauses)
- **Self-aware framing** (folder-naming, intellectual excitement confession)
- **Mock-reassurance** ("Nobody will be harmed except...")
- **Confident deployment** ("Crack it open and...")

### What to add more of:
1. **Bathetic deflation** — Build up to something grand, then puncture it
2. **Concrete absurdities** — Not abstract philosophical problems, but specific weird examples
3. **Mid-paragraph pivots** — A sentence that suddenly shifts register or reveals self-awareness
4. **Chapter-opening hooks** — Ch 5 does this well; Ch 3 doesn't

### Where NOT to add humour:
- The formal hyperreal material (Ch 5) — keep it clean
- Core diagnostic material (criteria tables, mechanism lists)
- Direct engagement with other scholars (Rapoport's Rules apply)

**Final verdict**: The book has a **good baseline** of humour (probably 25-30 moments across 17 chapters), but it's **unevenly distributed**. Chapters 1, 4, 8, 11 carry the load. Chapters 2, 3, and likely 9 are notably dry. The opportunities are **mid-chapter**, especially in transitions between heavy lifting and application, and in the **framing** of technical material (Skip paths, wastebasket openings, spinning top metaphors).
